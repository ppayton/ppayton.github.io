SENIOR PROJECT


FluffyMates: Swipe Right, Save a life

With a team, I developed an app called FluffyMates. The crossplatform FluffyMates app allows users to search any shelter for their next fluffy family member all in one place. Shelters are limited in resources often leaving their web and traditional advertisements missing photos, health, disposition information, and care needs of and about the animals. Shelters are often limited to advertising animals in the area, losing ground to breeders who advertise and sell nationally. FluffyMates includes pictures, medical history, and all other information about the animal. Users are able to search for animals all over the country to find the perfect addition to their family. Utilizing in app advertising will allow shelters and adopters to access the app at no cost.

For this project we used React Native and Amazon Web Services as our database. All pet information was pulled from Petfinder API.
